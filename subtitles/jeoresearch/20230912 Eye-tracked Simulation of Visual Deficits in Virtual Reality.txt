Title: Eye-tracked Simulation of Visual Deficits in Virtual Reality
Date: 20230912
Description:
This video shows research from Augusta University to simulate visual deficits in Virtual Reality. We use eye tracking to accurately replicate the experience of Macular Degeneration, Diabetic Retinopathy, Cataracts, Color Blindness, Myopia (nearsightedness), and Hyperopia (farsightedness). 

Our published work on this project can be found below: 
https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Jason-Orlosky/publication/341314909

Citation:
Alexander, D., Nguyen, T., Keller, P., Orlosky, J., Brown, S., Wood, E., Ezenwoye, O. and Jirau-Rosaly, W., 2020, March. Design of visual deficit simulation for integration into a geriatric physical diagnosis course. In 2020 IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces Abstracts and Workshops (VRW) (pp. 838-839). IEEE.
