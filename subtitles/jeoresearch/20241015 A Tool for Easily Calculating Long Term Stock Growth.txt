Title: A Tool for Easily Calculating Long Term Stock Growth
Date: 20241015
Description:
This is a simple tool that helps you plot and visualize long-term stock growth trends. It is designed with long-term investing in mind, and allows you to project stock growth from the current price to 10 years into the future by clicking on a point in the chart. 

The compounded growth rate is calculated based on historical data and can help estimate whether a given company or index is underpriced or overvalued based on long term trends. 

The tool is available on GitHub as both an EXE and Python script, and I’ve included a sample data file for 3M Corporation (Company). You can easily input your own stock data by downloading the historical data from Yahoo Finance in CSV format. 

Github repository (Python and .EXE): 
https://github.com/NuncObdurat/financialtools/tree/main/Stock%20Analysis

Please like and subscribe to help support this and other open source software! 

****Note that this tool is not a predictor of stock prices, but a calculator of the trend based on long-term growth.
