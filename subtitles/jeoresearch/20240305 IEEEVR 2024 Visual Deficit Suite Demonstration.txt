Title: IEEEVR 2024 Visual Deficit Suite Demonstration
Date: 20240305
Description:
In this demo, we present a suite of visual deficits implemented in both augmented and virtual reality to assist with education and diagnosis of ocular disorders, diseases, and deficits. More specifically, we use eye tracking to better simulate the difficulties in diseases such as macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, retinal tears, and other gaze-dependent deficits. We make use of both virtual reality (VR) and video see-through augmented reality (VST AR) to give users a unique perspective of life through the eyes of patients.

This video provides a more detailed explanation of our previous demonstration, found here: https://youtu.be/B1Bi3i-zFH4

Citation:
Orlosky, J., Bui., T., Winston, N., Jirau-Rosaly, W., and Brown, S. 2024, March. An Eye-tracked XR Visual Deficit Simulation Suite for Ocular Disease Education and Assisted Diagnosis. To appear in the 2024 IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces Abstracts and Workshops (VRW). IEEE.
