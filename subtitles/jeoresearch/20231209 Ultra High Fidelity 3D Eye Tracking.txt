Subtitles:

This video shows how eye tracking can be used to control high-fidelity eye models. After calibration, a 3D model helps fit the pupil ellipse to the correct position on the eye sphere. Then, the eye gaze ray and pupil radius are sent to Unity to control a 3D model. A custom shader modifies the pupil radius in real time. Stay tuned for details of how this is done.
