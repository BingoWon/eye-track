Title: Playing catch in the Varjo XR-3 (with the HP G2 VR backpack)
Date: 20230725
Description:
This video shows that the Varjo XR-3 head mounted display can be used outdoors to play catch. The XR-3 is a video see-through system with low enough latency to be able to handle high-paced, dynamic tasks.

Though we attached a power cable to preserve battery life, the system can be used outdoors completely untethered using the backpack's hot-swappable battery system.
