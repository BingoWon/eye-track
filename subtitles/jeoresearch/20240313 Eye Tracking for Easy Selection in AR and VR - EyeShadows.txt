Title: Eye Tracking for Easy Selection in AR and VR - EyeShadows
Date: 20240313
Description:
This video shows a method for easy selection and interaction using eye tracking. It's called "EyeShadows," and by creating virtual copies of objects or icons, you can easily select or modify settings for a variety of applications. 

This work will be presented at IEEEVR 2024, and is open-sourced at the following link: https://github.com/JEOresearch/EyeShadows

Drop me a line in the comments if you think of any interesting use cases for this technology! 

Reference: 
Orlosky, J., Liu., C, Sakamoto, K., Sidenmark, L., & Mansour, A. (2024). EyeShadows: Peripheral Virtual Copies for Rapid Gaze Selection and Interaction. To appear in the Proceedings of the IEEE conference on Virtual Reality.
