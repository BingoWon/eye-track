Title: Augmented Reality Cyclocomputers
Date: 20240330
Description:
Instead of having to look down, you can use augmented reality to attach content to the cyclist in front of you! This gives you a view of both your data and the person in front of you, and you can still easily tell when they are going to stop. 

The original system is described in our TVCG paper, which can be found below. 
https://drive.google.com/file/d/1CJ5r02zEU00B7pMmbpcqoPnLgSFM9YKt/view?usp=sharing

Citation:
Zhao, G., Orlosky, J., Gabbard, J., & Kiyokawa, K. (2023). HazARdSnap: Gazed-based Augmentation Delivery for Safe Information Access while Cycling. IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics.
